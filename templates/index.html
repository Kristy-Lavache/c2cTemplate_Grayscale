About

Knowing how hard it is to lose a loved one, we strive to make finding lost ones as simple as possible. With a few clicks of a button, we can help you locate where your loved ones are buried and any information we can find about them. We are sorry for your loss and if there is anything we can do to make your experience any easier, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here for you.

Soldier

Being a soldier is more than being in a uniform. Being a soldier means you are selfless as a leader. It means you are loyal, believe in your duty, have and are able to give respect, have honor, integrity, and personal courage. To be a soldier, one must have most if not all of these qualities. It is because of them that we are able to enjoy the future we get tomorrow. It is because of them we get to rejoice and be glad of our freedom. So, to all those individuals and soldiers, we say Thank You!

Oath

Soldiers take an oath to never leave a fallen behind and to never accept defeat. Today, we get to be soldiers. Never stop looking and always go back. We are here to help you search and find your loved one(s) so you can go back for them. Because for us, NO ONE gets left behind. Hoorah (HUA)! Oorah! Hooyah!
